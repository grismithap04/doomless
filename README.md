# doomless

**Break the doomscroll. Discover something new.**

A 3-hour hackathon project that transforms mindless scrolling into intentional activity discovery.

---

## 🎯 What is Doomless?

Doomless helps you escape the endless scroll by suggesting personalized activities based on your interests and available time. Instead of falling into the doomscroll trap, you get a curated activity suggestion, log what you actually do, and track your real-world engagement over time.

### The Flow

1. **Enter your available time** (e.g., "I have 30 minutes")
2. **Select your interests** from a checklist
3. **Get an AI-generated activity suggestion** via Gemini
4. **Click "Done"** to log the activity you completed
5. **View your activity history** to see what you've been up to
